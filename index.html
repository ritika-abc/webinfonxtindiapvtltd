    About Us

    Welcome to Om Gauri Global

    OM Gauri Enterprises, based in Telangana, India, is a leading exporter of agricultural products, specializing in items like Fox nuts (Makhana), millets, spices, mushrooms, and coconuts. With a strong commitment to delivering quality, the company ensures that its products meet international standards, catering to global markets. The company has established direct relationships with local farmers, ensuring high-grade products. By working closely with these producers, OM Gauri Enterprises ensures competitive pricing and prompt delivery to its customers worldwide. The business holds key certifications, including a valid Food and Safety license issued by the Government of India (License number: 13624999000519) and APEDA registration, solidifying its credibility in the export market.
